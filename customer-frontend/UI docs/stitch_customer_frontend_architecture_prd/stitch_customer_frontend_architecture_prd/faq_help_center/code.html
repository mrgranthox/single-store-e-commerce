Shipping

Do you offer international shipping? expand_more
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International orders typically arrive within 7-14 business days depending on customs processing in your region. Please note that duties and taxes are calculated at checkout for most destinations.
How can I track my order status? expand_more
Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to our tracking portal. You can also view status updates in your Nordic Commerce account under "Order History."

Returns

What is your return policy? expand_more
We offer free returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in their original condition, unworn, and with all tags attached. Nordic Home items must be returned in their original packaging.
How do I start a return? expand_more
Visit our Returns Portal and enter your order number and zip code. You will be able to print a prepaid shipping label and schedule a pickup or drop it off at any authorized courier location.

Sustainable Wool Care

Our premium wool garments are designed for longevity. We recommend hand washing with cold water and laying flat to dry to maintain the fiber integrity.

Technical Outerwear

Machine wash cold on a gentle cycle. Use a specialized technical detergent to preserve water-repellent coatings. Do not use fabric softeners.
