Our Heritage

Founded in a small basement workshop in Södermalm, The Digital Atelier began as a restoration project for mid-century furniture. Today, it stands as a global beacon for Nordic minimalism.

The Early Years

Our journey started with a singular focus: longevity. We believed that an object well-made should outlast its owner. This philosophy remains our guiding star.

The Digital Pivot

In 2018, we integrated algorithmic design with traditional joinery. This allowed us to scale our artisan principles without sacrificing the soul of the product.
