"How does the Stockholm Oak finish age over time with direct sunlight exposure?"

verified

Erik V., Product Design Head

Official Studio Response

Our Stockholm Oak is treated with a UV-resistant natural oil. Over 5-7 years, you will notice the honey tones deepen into a richer, warmer amber. We recommend a light re-oiling once every two years to maintain the structural moisture balance.
