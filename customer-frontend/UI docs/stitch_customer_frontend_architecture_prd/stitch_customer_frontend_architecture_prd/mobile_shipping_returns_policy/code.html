The Returns Policy

01

30-Day Window

Items must be returned within 30 days of the delivery date. We offer full refunds to the original payment method or store credit with a 10% bonus.

02

Original Condition

Products must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all architectural tags attached. Nordic reserves the right to deny returns that do not meet these standards.

03

Pre-Paid Labels

We provide a pre-paid return shipping label for all domestic orders. A $10 restocking fee will be deducted from your final refund total.

Still have questions?

Our concierge team is available 24/7 to assist with tracking, sizing, or return inquiries.
