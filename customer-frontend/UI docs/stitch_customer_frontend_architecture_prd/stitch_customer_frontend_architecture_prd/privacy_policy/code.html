Privacy Policy

At Nordic Commerce, we honor the simplicity and integrity of Scandinavian design in everything we do—including how we handle your personal data. This policy outlines our commitment to transparency and your privacy rights.

01 / Information Collection

We collect information that helps us provide a curated shopping experience. This includes direct identifiers like your name, email address, and shipping details provided during checkout or account creation.

person

Personal Identity

Name, contact info, and biographical details you share.

payments

Transaction Data

Order history and secure payment tokens (never raw card data).

02 / Use of Data

Your data isn't just numbers to us; it's the blueprint for a better service. We use your information to fulfill orders, personalize your editorial feed, and maintain the security of our atelier platform.

  • Processing and delivering your handcrafted selections.
  • Sending curated newsletters and story updates (only with your consent).
  • Improving site performance through anonymous behavioral analysis.

03 / Cookies & Tracking

We use essential and functional cookies to ensure our site feels as smooth as a polished oak surface. These small files help us remember your preferences and keep your shopping bag intact across sessions.

04 / Third-Party Sharing

We believe in quality over quantity. We only share data with trusted partners who are essential to our operations, such as logistics providers and secure payment gateways.

"We never sell your personal data to third-party advertisers. Our commerce is built on trust, not data brokering."

05 / Your Data Rights

You are the owner of your digital identity. Under global privacy standards, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you.
