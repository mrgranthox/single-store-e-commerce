# E-Commerce Customer Frontend Architecture (50-Screen Scope) ## Executive Overview This document defines the customer-facing frontend architecture for a single-store e-commerce system. It covers the full customer lifecycle: discovery, evaluation, purchase, account management, support, reviews, returns, and refunds. ## Design Principles - **Conversion with Clarity:** Reduce confusion and friction. - **Customer Confidence:** Trust signals (secure checkout, reviews, clear policies). - **Post-Purchase Excellence:** First-class treatment of orders, tracking, returns, and support. - **Mobile-First Usability:** Optimized for small screens. ## Screen Inventory (Summary) - **Storefront & Discovery (12):** Home, Shop, Categories, Search, Product Detail, Wishlist. - **Cart & Checkout (8):** Cart, Multi-step Checkout, Success, Guest Tracking. - **Auth & Identity (6):** Login, Register, Recovery. - **Account Hub (12):** Dashboard, Orders, Tracking, Returns, Refunds, Reviews, Security. - **Support & Service (6):** Support Center, Ticket Management, Help/FAQ. - **Content & Trust (6):** Contact, About, Policies (Shipping, Returns, Privacy, Terms). ## Implementation Phases 1. **Phase 1:** Shell, Home, Catalog, Product Detail, Cart/Wishlist, Auth Basics. 2. **Phase 2:** Full Checkout Flow & Success. 3. **Phase 3:** Account Dashboard, Orders & Tracking. 4. **Phase 4:** Returns, Refunds, Reviews, Support Center. 5. **Phase 5:** Campaign pages, Preferences, and Policy Content.
